    INBOX

    Goal(s)

  • Help the pro know:

    • Exactly where they can find each type of lead

    • Get the pro to set their preferences, including the budget.

    • The best practices for winning a lead.

    • Action Button


    Talking Points

    1. "Let's hop over to your inbox...This is where you'll see two types of leads show up."

    2. "Exact Matches (Leads)"

      • "The first and most valuable kind of lead are the exact matches, so you'll want to make sure your noifications care set correctly so you can respons to them in a timely manner. I can show you how to do that if you'd like."
        • "When the lead says "exact match" this means that it's a perfect matche because you told us what you always want in your preferences. These are also leads you've already paid for.



    3. Partial Matches (Leads)

      • "The other type of lead is a partial match. These don't match your preferences perfectly, so you'll have the option to accept or decline it."

      • "You'll want to accept or delcine these leads quickly because this counts toward your response time, which affects where you rank in the search results."

      • "It might be the job type that's a little off or the distance bay be a little further than you'd like...but this customer still reached out to you, so it's still a pretty good lead...so you'll want to respond to thse quickly, too."

      • "The good news, since these kind of leads don't match your preferences perfectly, you can choose to accept the lead, you'll pay full price for this lead (since it doesn't matche your preferences perfectly) or you can decline it and not pay for it...just make sure you let the customer know why you declined it...we want to provide the best customer experience possible."

    4. "Communication Tips"

      • On top of responding quickly, I want to give you 6 pointers on the best practces to wain a lead:

        1. Use the customer's name.

        2. Be excited and thankful they reached out to you.

        3. Answer questions the customer asked.

        4. Address the job details the customer provided

        5. Ask open ended questions.

        6. Communicate when you're available and that you're excited to connect.

    CLOSING

    Goal(s)

  • Recaps and summarizes the call.
  • Review action items for pro to focus on
    1. Let them know we'll send an email with top profile tips
  • Schedule a follow-up call (GOAL is 1 week or less out)
    1. We'll review profile in more depth during second call.
    2. Pro accepts invite so it blocks off time.

    • Action Button


    Talking Points

    1. "Now that your preferences are dialed...the most important thing you should do now is finish getting your profile set up." (emphasize Reviews and that they should start getting them asap. Compliment the work they've already done and, if time allows, touch on some of the other elements of the profile.)

      • Introduction: The introduction section of your profile is where you give customers plenty of reasons to hire you. A great introduction explains your qualifications - What makes you unique and shows your personality.

      • Reviews: Before a customer decides to hire you, they want to know what it's like to work with you. And reviews give them an opportunity to hear from some of your past customers.

      • Pictures and Videos: Add photos or videos to your profile to highlight your past work. Give customers a sense of what you can do. 83% of pros hired on Thumbtack have at least one picture highlighting their work.

      • Background Check & Licenses: Build trust by completing a background check and listing your professional licenses.

      • FAQs: Answer the FAQ section of your profile to prove to customers what we already know - how great you are. These questions give customers a closer look into wht it's like to work with you and what makes you stand out.


    2. "I will be sending you a follow-up email with everything we covered today as well as those next steps for your profile."

    3. "Let's chat again in 2-3 days to make sure you're set up to talk to a success manager. They will be creating a success plan for you and will be your point of contact once you're all set up."

    4. I'm available on [2-3 date options] what works best for you?

    5. Thanks so much for taking the time to speak with me today. I'm really excited to see how this works for you, have a great day, [pro name].
